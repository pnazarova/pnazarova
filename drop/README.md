# Drop folder

Dump anything related to Greg's website here — nothing in this folder is published, it's raw material for rebuilding the site.

## What's useful

**`photos/`** — any images of Greg
- Headshots (the higher resolution the better)
- Photos of him teaching, speaking, on stage
- Podcast cover art / unSILOed logo
- Any logos we're allowed to use (Berkeley, Stanford, program logos)

**`docs/`** — any documents
- CV / resume (any format)
- Bio versions (short bio, long bio, speaker intro)
- Speaker one-pagers or program brochures
- Testimonials, reviews, press quotes

**`links.md`** — paste URLs
- His profile pages (Haas, Stanford, LinkedIn, etc.)
- Talks on YouTube, podcast episodes worth featuring
- Articles by or about him
- Websites whose design you like as reference

**`notes.md`** — freeform
- What the site is *for* (booking speaking gigs? credibility page? podcast growth?)
- What you/Greg like or dislike about the current version and the Netlify one
- Color/style preferences, must-have and must-not-have sections
- The domain you plan to use, contact email to publish (if any)

## What happens next

Once there's material here, the site gets rebuilt around it — real photos in the hero and about sections, bio text taken from his actual documents, and structure driven by what the site needs to accomplish.
